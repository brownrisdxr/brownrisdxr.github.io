About

We’re a student club at Brown and RISD exploring XR (extended reality) technologies. Our members come from diverse backgrounds in design, art, engineering, and research. All are welcome.

Projects

We build, we prototype, we experiment. Past work includes VR art installations, Unity games, and XR-for-health initiatives. More coming soon.
